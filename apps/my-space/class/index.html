Reflective Practice

1

Description

What happened? Describe the situation objectively. Who was involved? What was the context? What were you trying to achieve? Avoid judgements at this stage.

2

Feelings

What were you thinking and feeling? Before, during, and after the event. How did your feelings influence the situation? What do you think others were feeling?

3

Evaluation

What was good and bad about the experience? What worked? What didn't? What was the high point and the low point? Assess the situation honestly.

4

Analysis

What sense can you make of the situation? Why did things go well or poorly? What sense can you make of the situation? Does your previous knowledge explain this?

5

Conclusion

What else could you have done? What did you learn from this? What skills did you use or miss? If it happened again, how would you approach it differently?

6

Action Plan

If this arose again, what would you do? What training or skills do you need? How will you prepare for next time? What specific steps will you take?
